Publications

Enhancing Breast Cancer Classification Through GAN-Generated Synthetic Malignant Samples

IEEE FMLDS, 2025

K. Balakrishna, A. Baisakh, V. Kanna, A. Lee, M. Al-Wishah

Proposed a GAN-based minority-class augmentation approach for tabular clinical data using the UCI Wisconsin Breast Cancer dataset. Synthetic malignant samples were used to balance the training set for a soft-voting ensemble classifier.

Validation Accuracy: 98.07% · F1-score: 97.40%

ALTARN: A Tabular Attention Residual Network for Early Alzheimer’s Disease Prediction

IEEE FMLDS, 2025

K. Balakrishna, A. Baisakh, V. Kanna, M. Al-Wishah, A. Lee

Proposed a tabular attention residual neural network for Alzheimer’s disease classification using heterogeneous medical, demographic, and lifestyle data from 2,149 subjects.

Validation Accuracy: 85.06% · Precision: 80.73% · Recall: 76.18% · F1-score: 78.32%
